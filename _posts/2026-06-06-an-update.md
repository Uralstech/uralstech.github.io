so, to get started, i've recently pushed a couple of commits to [UMoth](https://github.com/Uralstech/UMoth/) to keep it up to date with Unity 6.4's Gradle and AGP updates. i also updated its native dependencies. since these are the first commits to the repo in like 9 months, and considering some of my other repos have also not been updated in a while, i thought i should clarify how much i've been working on my packages.

all of my packages are maintained primarily based on the needs of my own / my company's projects, but i do love delving into feature requests when possible. the most recent update to [UXR.QuestCamera](https://github.com/Uralstech/UXR.QuestCamera/releases/tag/4.1.0) is borne from a question regarding [exposure control](https://github.com/Uralstech/UXR.QuestCamera/issues/14) using the package. getting into it allowed me to explore the Camera2 API better, and, as a plus for my other projects, now allows me to control the camera's framerate for better performance.
